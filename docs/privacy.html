Dovi Bot

Privacy Policy

Effective July 13, 2026

Information processed

Dovi Bot may process and store Discord user, server, channel, message, and interaction identifiers; usernames; command names, options, results, and error details; stream settings and reminders; keyword notification preferences; boss tracking, polls, nominations, and votes; and related timestamps.

For enabled community features, the bot may process message text to match personal notification keywords, collect community-topic statistics, or support moderation logging. Keyword matching does not require storing the matched message text. Topic statistics retain identifiers, timestamps, a content hash, and detected topic signals. If message logging is enabled by the operator, recent or deleted message text and author information may also be stored.

How information is used

Information is used to operate requested commands and features, deliver notifications and reminders, maintain community statistics and bot-tracked records, diagnose errors, prevent abuse, and improve reliability.

Sharing

The bot does not sell personal information. Information is shared only with service providers needed to operate the bot, such as Discord and database or hosting providers, or when required by law or necessary to protect users and the service.

Retention and security

Information is kept only while reasonably needed for the features described above, operational records, security, or legal obligations. Reasonable safeguards are used, but no online service can guarantee absolute security.

Your choices

You can stop using the bot. Server administrators can remove it from their server. Where supported, bot commands let users change or clear their preferences. You may also contact the operator to request access to or deletion of personal information, subject to legal, security, and technical limitations.

Children

Dovi Bot is not intended for anyone below the minimum age required to use Discord in their country.

Changes

This policy may be updated when the bot or its data practices change. The effective date above will be revised when material changes are published.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact the Dovi Bot operator in the Discord server where the bot is installed.
